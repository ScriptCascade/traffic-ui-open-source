Install Traffic-UI
==================

One-line install:
bash <(curl -Ls https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ScriptCascade/traffic-ui-open-source/main/install.sh)

OR

Simple way:
sudo bash install.sh

OR

Detailed way:
chmod +x install-traffic-ui.sh
sudo ./install-traffic-ui.sh

Uninstall Traffic-UI
====================
bash <(curl -Ls https://rebrand.ly/wu3c0wg)
